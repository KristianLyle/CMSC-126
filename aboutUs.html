Our Mission

Our mission is to promote a deeper understanding and appreciation of the precolonial history of the Philippines, with a focus on decolonizing and deconstructing the Eurocentric narratives that have dominated Philippine history. We aim to highlight the rich and diverse cultural heritage of the various Indigenous peoples that inhabited the archipelago long before the arrival of the Spanish colonizers. Through our website, we strive to provide a platform for marginalized voices and perspectives, and to encourage a more inclusive and equitable approach to historical inquiry and education. By challenging the colonial legacy that still shapes the way we view Philippine history today, we hope to contribute to a more just and compassionate society that values and respects the dignity and agency of all its members.
